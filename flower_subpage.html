Falling Flower

Houdini, Karma, Solaris

2024

I am currently taking a virtual cinematography course at DePaul University. In which, I've decided to continue with learning Houdini and expand my abilities. For this project, we were tasked with utilizing camera movements to emphasize actions within a shot. Focusing on a flower that looses its pedals, this project introduced me to utilizing camera movements. Additionally, I continued to work on my VEX skills that allowed for the falling effect.
