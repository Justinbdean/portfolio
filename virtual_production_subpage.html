The Cave: Virtual Production

Unreal Engine

2024

Over the summer, I have the incredible opportunity to work on a virtual production short film. I led a small team where we built a virtual environment and aided in on-set technical support for the virtual volume. I specifically worked on assembling the scene within Unreal Engine from assets built by my team members and from Quixel Bridge. I had only just touched Unreal for the first time in January of 2024 so this was a big confidence boost in what I was capable of. We were able to film the short film at SMASH Studio in Chicago, Illinois which was a fantastic experience. This film is still in production and once is complete will be added to this page.
